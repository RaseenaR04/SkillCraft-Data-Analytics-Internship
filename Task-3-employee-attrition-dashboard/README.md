## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of **Task 3 – Interactive Visualization** in the SkillCraft Data Analyst Internship.

The objective of this task was to create an interactive dashboard using Tableau to analyze employee attrition data and identify key factors contributing to workforce turnover.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To design an interactive dashboard that:

- Analyzes employee attrition patterns  
- Allows filtering by department, age group, and other attributes  
- Answers key business questions such as:
  - Why are employees leaving?
  - Which factors influence attrition the most?

---

## 📂 Dataset Used

**HR Employee Attrition Dataset**

The dataset includes employee-related attributes such as:

- Age  
- Department  
- Job Role  
- Monthly Income  
- Overtime  
- Education  
- Attrition Status

## 🔎 Key Insights

- Research & Development department shows relatively higher attrition.
- Employees working overtime have significantly higher turnover.
- Employees aged 25–35 show increased attrition rates.
- Employees who left tend to have lower median monthly income.

---

## 💼 Business Impact

The dashboard helps HR teams to:

- Identify high-risk employee segments  
- Understand key attrition drivers  
- Develop retention strategies  
- Make data-driven workforce decisions
